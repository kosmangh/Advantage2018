Non-functional requirements

Non-functional requirements are constraints on the services or functions offered by the system. They include timing constraints, constraints on the development process, and constraints imposed by standards. Some of the various non-functional requirements of the system are:

  1. Users should login to gain access to the system
  2. Print various reports
  3. Users are presented with views depending on the various roles assigned which are administrator, director, estate officer, accountant and auditor.
