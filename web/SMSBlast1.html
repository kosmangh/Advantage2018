SMS Blast

This section enables sending of sms to various contact groups.These contact groups can be all occupants,estates,blocks or individuals.


To be able to send a sms reminders involves:

1. First selecting a contact group

2. Add a title

3.Type in the message you want to send in the area provided

4. Click on the 'Send' button to send the sms to the chosen recipients
